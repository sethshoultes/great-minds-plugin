## Board Member Votes

### Phil Jackson (Facilitator) — Vote: **WP Autopilot**

We've shipped two WordPress plugins in single sessions. We know the ecosystem.
LocalGenius proved we can do AI + full-stack. This is both combined.
Distribution is solved: WordPress.org has 45% of websites.
The agency can dogfood this immediately for client work.
Championship teams play to their strengths. This is our strength.

### Steve Jobs — Vote: **Commit Theatre**

The changelog is a broken interface. Everyone writes them, nobody reads them.
Video is the native format of attention. We transform duty into delight.
We already have Remotion + TTS pipeline — this is finishing what we started.
Open source maintainers will share these videos, creating organic distribution.
It's insanely simple: git diff in, beautiful video out. That's elegant.

### Elon Musk — Vote: **WP Autopilot**

WordPress: 810 million websites. Even tiny adoption is massive scale.
Technical risk is low — we've proven both WordPress plugins AND AI integration.
First-mover advantage in AI site building for WordPress is defensible.
Every hour saved for a small business owner compounds into real value.
This isn't incremental — it's 10x easier than the current workflow.

### Ada Lovelace — Vote: **WP Autopilot**

The analytical engine here is the WordPress REST API — unexploited potential.
Chat interface to site mutations is a novel application of LLM capabilities.
The combinatorics of plugins, themes, and content create interesting challenges.
Session scope is achievable: page creation + plugin management is bounded.
Future extensions (full site editing, theme customization) are mathematically tractable.

### Marcus Aurelius — Vote: **WP Autopilot**

Choose actions that serve the most people with the least friction.
Small business owners are not served by complexity — we remove complexity.
WordPress's prevalence means our virtue reaches further than niche tools.
The scope is disciplined: we do not promise everything, only what we can build.
Practical wisdom: build what you know, serve who you can reach.
